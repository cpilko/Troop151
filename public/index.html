About Us

We meet at 7pm on Wednesdays during the school year in Fellowship Hall at Marple Presbyterian Church (Map)

Each month our goal is to have two outings: One campout and one other event.

We are Scout-Led: Our Scouts develop our program, and our adult leaders help them make it happen.

What We Do

Camping

We are not a club or a Sunday school class, but a school of the woods.

Community Involvement

We must change boys from a ‘what can I get’ to a ‘what can I give’ attitude.

Marksmanship

The Scout is not governed by don’t, but is led by do.

STEM

A fisherman does not bait his hook with food he likes. He uses food the fish likes. So with Scouts.

Service

The real way to gain happiness is to give it to others.

Patriotism

Loyalty is a feature in a boy’s character that inspires boundless hope.

Winning

It is important to arrange games and competition so that all Scouts of the troop take part.

Learning

In Scouting, a Scout is encouraged to educate himself instead of being instructed.

Hiking

Where is there a boy to whom the call of the wild and the open road does not appeal?

Summer Camp

A week of camp life is worth six months of theoretical teaching in the meeting room.

Fun and Games

Vigorous Scout games are the best form of physical education because most of them bring in moral education.

First Aid

The most important object in Boy Scout training is to educate, not instruct

All quotes by Lord Baden-Powell, the founder of Scouting
